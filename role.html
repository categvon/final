PROFESSIONAL ROLE

Job Description

The Principal Librarian, Research & Special Collections role at the Los Angeles Public Library is a senior leadership position that involves overseeing rare books, archives, and digital collections within a public library system. The duties associated with this specialization include library administration, archival stewardship, grant development, exhibitions and programming, and technology and systems management.
